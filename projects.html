Research Assistant — Cognitive Model in LLM

New York University · Shanghai, China · Jun 2024 – Dec 2024

Advisor: Prof. Hongyi Wen (Assistant Professor)

  • Engineered a World Values Survey pipeline using embeddings + K-Means, boosting cultural-cognitive segmentation (silhouette score ↑ ~200% with Sentence Transformers vs. baseline).
  • Applied Prompt Optimization (ProTeGi) with gradient-based updates and chain-of-thought feedback, reducing value-judgment prediction error (MSE ↓ ~6%) and improving contextual alignment.
  • Fine-tuned LLaMA-3 with LoRA for cognitive decision tasks, achieving >90% parameter reduction versus full fine-tuning while validating performance across clusters.
